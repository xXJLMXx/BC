The mission of the Bakersfield College Student Health and Wellness Services at Bakersfield College is to further the health equity of the educational opportunity. This is accomplished by providing access to high value, student centered and culturally competent health services. Services which promote the physical, emotional and spiritual wellness of its students and their academic environment. This wellness contributes to the educational aim of our college by promoting student persistence and academic success in congruence with the college’s mission, core values and strategic directions.

Bakersfield College Student Health and Wellness Services is committed to providing all students with quality health care. We hope you find the Student Health Portal to be a useful tool to help manage your health.

In the event you become injured or ill on campus or witness an injury, contact Public Safety at (661) 395-4554. Public Safety will advise you of what to do next. When in doubt about a life or death circumstance or serious injury or illness call 911 and then call Public Safety at (661) 395-4554.
